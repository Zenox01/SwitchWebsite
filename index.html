Notify Me

With Notify Me service, users will receive a text message when the other user is available on the network, notifying them whether the called party was out of coverage or their phone was switched off.

Sponsor Me

The service allows you the liberty of making calls while you are out of balance to anybody you want, while a close one pays for it. Service subscriber would add the sponsor after subscribing the service. Sponsor would be sent the SMS for verification and authentication. Once verified then customer will be able to call using the balance of his sponsor.

Smart SMS

Easy Call

This service is for when your battery is draining but you need to make a call, it allows you to use party B’s phone to make a call, but your balance is deducted and receiving party is shown your number instead of Party B’s.

The VIP Call

With the VIP Call service , one can manage calls for a specific number, i.e. getting calls from desired callers only and transferring others to an designated MSISDN. People can’t call on the number without the owners consent i.e knowing their password. The owner of the number can transfer calls to managers/secretaries. This service is useful for high profile users such as celebrities and politicians .
